What areas do you serve?+
We serve Manassas, Woodbridge, and surrounding Northern Virginia communities. Not sure if we cover your zip code? Give us a call at (703) 555-0182 and we'll let you know!
How far in advance should I book?+
We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and holidays. That said, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests - just give us a call!
Is delivery and setup included?+
Yes! Delivery, setup, and takedown are always included. We handle everything so you can focus on enjoying the party. We confirm the day before with exact arrival times.
Can I mix and match items?+
Absolutely! We're always flexible. Mix and match any items to build your perfect party. Contact us for a custom quote.
What if it rains on my event day?+
Safety comes first. If there's severe weather, we'll work with you to reschedule at no extra charge. Tents and other items may still be available.
How do I book?+
Call or text (703) 555-0182, or send us a message on Facebook at @pepes.party.rentals. We'll confirm availability, discuss what you need, and lock in your date!
