Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your event with Pepe's Party Rental.

How do I book a rental with Pepe's Party Rental? ▼
Booking is simple and 100% online. Head to our website, choose your equipment, pick your date, and confirm your reservation in minutes — no phone calls required, no deposit needed. You'll receive a booking confirmation right away, and we'll give you a confirmation call two days before your event to lock in all the details.
How far in advance do I need to book? ▼
We accept bookings as little as 2 days before your event. While booking early guarantees your preferred equipment and date, we understand plans change fast — and we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests based on availability. When in doubt, reach out and we'll tell you exactly what we can make happen.
Is there a deposit required to reserve my rental? ▼
No deposit is required when you book online. Simply select your equipment, confirm your date, and your reservation is secured. We make it as easy as possible to book with zero upfront commitment.
What's included — do I have to set anything up myself? ▼
Never. Every rental includes professional delivery, full setup, and complete teardown by our team. We arrive on time, install everything safely, and return at the end of your event to handle all the cleanup. All you have to do is enjoy the party.
Where do you deliver? Do you deliver to parks? ▼
We deliver to private homes and residential properties within 50 miles of Stafford, Virginia. At this time we do not deliver to public parks or public venues. Not sure if your address is in our coverage area? Reach out and we'll confirm right away.
Are your inflatables and equipment safe and clean? ▼
Absolutely. Every unit is cleaned, sanitized, and safety-inspected before every delivery. With 15+ years in the business, safety is the foundation of everything we do — 97% of our customers rate their experience 5 stars and 99% of our events are delivered on schedule.
What happens if the weather is bad on my event day? ▼
Light rain typically doesn't stop us. If severe weather creates genuinely unsafe conditions, we'll work with you to reschedule at our earliest available date. We communicate with you in the days leading up to your event so you're never caught off guard — and we'll never leave you without a plan.
What if something breaks or stops working during my party? ▼
Call us immediately — our team is reachable throughout your entire rental. If there's a malfunction on our end, we'll troubleshoot remotely or send someone out as fast as possible. We've been doing this for 15+ years and we take our commitment seriously: 95% of customers say they'd rent from us again.
How much space and power do I need for an inflatable? ▼
Most standard MooBounces need approximately 15×15 ft of flat, clear space with a few feet of clearance on all sides. You'll also need a standard 110V outlet within reach — we bring our own extension cords. Not sure if your setup works? Share your space details before booking and we'll confirm everything fits.
Do you offer service in Spanish? / ¿Ofrecen servicio en español? ▼
Yes — hablamos español con gusto. From booking to delivery, our team can assist you fully in both English and Spanish so nothing gets lost in translation.
What equipment does Pepe's Party Rental offer? ▼
We offer MooBounces (bounce houses and inflatables), The Beast™ mechanical bull, event tents, tables and chairs, and party supplies — everything you need for an unforgettable backyard celebration. Whether it's a birthday, quinceañera, backyard BBQ, or family gathering, we've got you covered.
Why choose Pepe's Party Rental over other companies? ▼
We're a family-owned business with 15+ years serving families across the Stafford, Virginia area. Our numbers speak for themselves: 97% 5-star reviews, 99% on-time delivery, and 95% of customers come back. We show up on time, set up professionally, and treat every event like it's our own family's celebration — because that's exactly how we started.
